My Thesis

For my thesis, I am working with Professor Arun Chattopadhyay from the Department of Chemistry, IIT Guwahati.



Arun Sir's Lab

We're exploiting the SERS phenomenon in the detection of target molecules present in concentrations as small as in the nanomolar range. During the first phase of the project, I learnt how to synthesise gold nanoparticles and gold nanorods, as well as verify that they were indeed what I wanted to synthesise.
