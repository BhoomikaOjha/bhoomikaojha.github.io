The Excited State

The Excited State is CheSTA's (Chemical Science and Technology Association's) magazine. Last year, we released our first edition!


Take A Look!

This magazine's initial target audience was the department of chemistry - the faculty, undergrads, postgrads and post-doc candidates. We pulled this off with a team of 8, and I had an amazing time being an integral part of the writing, editing and designing process.

This time, we're a much bigger team, and we're trying to expand our horizons! This year, the world celebrated 150 glorious years of the periodic table and our next edition will be dedicated to it.

We have a steadily growing social media presence - stay tuned for updates!
